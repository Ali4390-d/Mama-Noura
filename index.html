Our Story

A Table Set With Memory

Mama Noura began in a small Riyadh kitchen in 1998, where Noura herself cooked for neighbors on Fridays. The smell of slow-roasted lamb and freshly ground cardamom became a weekly ritual — and then a restaurant.

Three generations later, we still grind our own spices every morning, still bake bread in the same clay oven, and still welcome every guest the way Mama Noura welcomed her first neighbor — with extra rice, with extra love.
